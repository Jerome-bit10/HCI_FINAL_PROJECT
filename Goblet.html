My Favorite Movie of All Time

Harry Potter and The Goblet of Fire

(Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire follows Harry’s fourth year at Hogwarts, where the school hosts the dangerous Triwizard Tournament. Despite being underage, Harry is mysteriously chosen as a competitor alongside Cedric Diggory, Fleur Delacour, and Viktor Krum. The three tasks test courage and skill: battling a dragon, rescuing friends from the Black Lake, and navigating a deadly maze. In the final task, Harry and Cedric are transported to a graveyard where Voldemort regains his full strength and kills Cedric. Harry narrowly escapes with the help of his parents’ spirits, returning to Hogwarts with the grim truth that Voldemort has returned, setting the stage for darker times ahead. )
