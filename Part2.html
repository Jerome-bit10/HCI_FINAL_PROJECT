My Favorite Movie of All Time

Harry Potter and The Deathly Hallows Part 2

(In Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, Voldemort’s return to power casts a shadow over both the wizarding and Muggle worlds. At the start, Severus Snape makes an Unbreakable Vow to Narcissa Malfoy, promising to protect her son Draco, who has been tasked with a dangerous mission by Voldemort. Meanwhile, Dumbledore recruits Horace Slughorn to teach at Hogwarts, hoping to extract a crucial memory about Voldemort’s past. Throughout the year, Harry works closely with Dumbledore, learning about Horcruxes—objects containing fragments of Voldemort’s soul, which must be destroyed to defeat him. Harry also discovers a mysterious potions textbook filled with helpful notes from the “Half-Blood Prince.” The story culminates when Death Eaters infiltrate Hogwarts, and Draco hesitates to kill Dumbledore. Snape steps in and fulfills the vow by killing Dumbledore himself. This shocking betrayal leaves Harry determined to continue the fight against Voldemort by hunting down and destroying the Horcruxes. )
