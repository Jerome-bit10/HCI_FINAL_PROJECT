My Favorite Movie of All Time

Harry Potter and The Philosopher Stone

(In Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone, we follow Harry’s first year at Hogwarts. After a miserable childhood with the Dursleys, Harry learns from Hagrid that he is a wizard and enrolls at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. There, he befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and discovers he is already famous in the wizarding world for surviving Lord Voldemort’s attack as a baby. Throughout the year, the trio uncovers the mystery of the Philosopher’s Stone, a magical object that grants immortality. They suspect someone is trying to steal it, and their investigation leads them through a series of magical challenges. In the final confrontation, Harry faces Professor Quirrell, who is possessed by Voldemort. With courage and the protection of his mother’s love, Harry prevents Voldemort from obtaining the Stone, which is later destroyed to keep it safe from misuse. )
