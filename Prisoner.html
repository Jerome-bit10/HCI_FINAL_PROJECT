My Favorite Movie of All Time

Harry Potter and The Prisoner Azkaban

(In Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban, Harry begins his third year at Hogwarts under the shadow of Sirius Black, a dangerous prisoner who has escaped from Azkaban. Rumors spread that Black is after Harry, and the school is guarded by terrifying Dementors. As the year unfolds, Harry learns more about his parents’ past and discovers that Sirius is not the villain he was led to believe, but his godfather who was wrongly imprisoned. With the help of Hermione’s Time-Turner, Harry and Hermione go back in time to save Sirius from capture and rescue Buckbeak the hippogriff from execution. The story ends with Sirius escaping on Buckbeak, leaving Harry with the hope of a family connection and a deeper understanding of loyalty and truth. )
